Todo Pro Max

This is a collaborative project among a group of developers: Alao Olamide(Team Lead),Jinad Temitope, Adeosun Samuel, Aderibigbe Victor and Adeshola Ayomide who formed the idea of creating a TodoProMax. TodoProMax is designed to help individuals and teams to manage tasks and projects effectively, offering features such as task assignment and deadline tracking. The creators aimed to make the tool user-friendly and customizable, so that it could be used by individuals of all fields.
